CHEST X-RAY

The site is used to analyze X-ray images. It is trained with labeled data to recognize patterns associated with normal, bacterial, or viral conditions. Based on the image, the system classifies it as "Normal", "Bacterial" or "Viral." The results are displayed through a user-friendly web interface that is easy to access and understand.

Brain Tumor MRI

The site is designed to analyze brain tumor MRI images. It is trained with labeled datasets containing MRI scans categorized as "Glioma", "Meningioma", "No Tumor" or "Pituitary." The system uses deep learning algorithms to identify patterns and features characteristic of each category.

ISIC MELANOMA

The site is also used to analyze images of melanoma. Based on the image, the system classifies it as "Melanoma", "Keratosis" or "Nevus".
